LEGENDARY COMPOSER

Nobuo Uematsu

Joined Square in 1985 as a self-taught musician. His iconic compositions have defined an era of Japanese RPGs. From "One-Winged Angel" to "No Promises to Keep" — his music is the very soul of Final Fantasy VII.

FROM THE DIRECTOR

"When we began reimagining Final Fantasy VII, we asked ourselves a single question: what would it mean to truly do this story justice for both the fans who have carried it in their hearts for nearly three decades, and the new generation experiencing it for the very first time? The answer shaped everything — every design decision, every line of dialogue, every note of music. This trilogy is not a retelling. It is a re-living. We wanted players to feel the weight of every choice, the depth of every bond, and the wonder of a world that has only grown richer with time. Our journey with Cloud and his companions is reaching its final chapter, and I can promise you — the destination is worth every step."

Naoki Hamaguchi

Game Director — Final Fantasy VII Remake Trilogy

THE JOURNEY

A Story 30 Years in the Making

1997

The Legend Begins

Original FFVII released on PlayStation. Sold 10M+ copies on PS1 alone, revolutionizing the RPG genre worldwide.

2005

Advent Children

Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children CGI film reignites global demand. Fans begin an eight-year wait for a proper remake.

2015

E3 Announcement

Remake officially announced at E3 2015. The internet erupts. A decade-long dream becomes reality.

2017

In-House Development

Development moved fully in-house at Square Enix. No original documentation survived — the team rebuilt from memory.

2020

FF7 Remake Arrives

Released April 10, 2020. Won Best RPG and Best Score & Music at The Game Awards. 8.7M copies sold worldwide.

2021

Intergrade

Enhanced PS5/PC version with the INTERmission DLC — Yuffie's story — expanding the world before Rebirth.

2024

FF7 Rebirth Soars

Released February 29, 2024. 40+ GOTY awards. Highest-scored game of the year on Metacritic. 125+ perfect scores.

2026

Platform Expansion

Remake and Rebirth simultaneously launch on Nintendo Switch 2 and Xbox Series X|S on June 3, bringing the trilogy to all.

2027

FF7 Revelation — The Final Chapter

Spring 2027. The trilogy concludes. Day-one on all platforms. The fate of Cloud, Aerith, and the Planet is finally revealed.
